🚨 Common Problems

Here are the most frequently reported issues from customers:

🔐 Login Issues

Users face trouble logging in due to incorrect credentials, expired sessions, or account lockouts.

💳 Payment Not Reflected

Transaction completed but the amount isn't reflected in their account. May be due to delay or gateway error.

⚠️ App Crash / Bug

App crashes during checkout or while navigating. Most common on Android version 9 and below.

💰 Request for Refund

Refunds requested for failed services or incorrect charges. Often accompanied by tickets or email escalation.
